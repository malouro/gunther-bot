<p>The <b>Calendar</b> is a piece of <a href="/Furniture" title="Furniture">furniture</a> that hangs on a wall.  <span style="display:inline-block; padding:0px 3px; border-radius:4px; border-style:outset; background:#FFFFFF; background: -webkit-linear-gradient(#FCFCFC, #E0E0E0); background: linear-gradient(#FCFCFC, #E0E0E0);">Right-clicking</span> on the calendar shows all birthdays and <a href="/Festivals" title="Festivals">Festivals</a> of the current <a href="/Seasons" title="Seasons">season</a>.
