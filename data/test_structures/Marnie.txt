<p><b>Marnie</b> is a <a href="https://stardewcommunitywiki.com/Villagers" title="Villagers">villager</a> who lives at <a href="https://stardewcommunitywiki.com/Marnie%27s_Ranch" title="Marnie's Ranch">Marnie's Ranch</a> in the northeast area of <a href="https://stardewcommunitywiki.com/Cindersap_Forest" title="Cindersap Forest">Cindersap Forest</a>, near the southwest entrance to <a href="https://stardewcommunitywiki.com/Pelican_Town" title="Pelican Town">Pelican Town</a>.
</p><p>Marnie runs her own shop at Marnie's Ranch from 9:00 AM to 4:00 
PM each day, except Monday and Tuesday, although the building is still 
open on these days. She sells <a href="https://stardewcommunitywiki.com/Animals" title="Animals">animals</a> and animal supplies.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Relationships">Relationships</span></h2>
<p>Marnie lives with her niece, <a href="https://stardewcommunitywiki.com/Jas" title="Jas">Jas</a>, and her nephew, <a href="https://stardewcommunitywiki.com/Shane" title="Shane">Shane</a>.  When it is not raining, she exercises with <a href="https://stardewcommunitywiki.com/Caroline" title="Caroline">Caroline</a>, <a href="https://stardewcommunitywiki.com/Jodi" title="Jodi">Jodi</a>, <a href="https://stardewcommunitywiki.com/Emily" title="Emily">Emily</a>, and <a href="https://stardewcommunitywiki.com/Robin" title="Robin">Robin</a> on Tuesdays at <a href="https://stardewcommunitywiki.com/Pierre%27s_General_Store" title="Pierre's General Store">Pierre's General Store</a>.
</p><p><a href="https://stardewcommunitywiki.com/Lewis" title="Lewis">Mayor Lewis</a> and Marnie have a very close relationship. Although Marnie claims to be single during the <a href="https://stardewcommunitywiki.com/Flower_Dance" title="Flower Dance">Flower Dance</a>,
 Lewis and Marnie's 6-heart event confirms that they are dating.  Lewis 
visits Marnie's Ranch to attend to her "business needs" on occasion, and
 the two can often be found drinking together at <a href="https://stardewcommunitywiki.com/The_Stardrop_Saloon" title="The Stardrop Saloon">the Stardrop Saloon</a> in the evening.  In one of the <a href="https://stardewcommunitywiki.com/Quests#Mayor.27s_.E2.80.9CShorts.E2.80.9D" title="Quests">story quests</a>, Lewis asks you to find his lucky purple shorts, which can be found in Marnie's bedroom.
</p>
